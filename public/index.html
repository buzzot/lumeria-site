Lumeria is a small atelier of designers working in glass, brass, stone and paper. Each piece is made in editions of fewer than two hundred.

The catalogue

02

The studio

Lumeria works from a converted boatyard on the Adriatic. The studio is small on purpose: four designers, eight makers, a single shared kiln. Pieces are signed, numbered, and shipped in handmade crates.

We work in cycles of two seasons. Each catalogue is closed when its editions are complete; nothing is reprinted. What you see here is what remains.
